ABOUT US

Alpha Helix Energy is an independent Oil and Natural Gas producer located in the heart of West Virginia's Appalachian basin. Alpha Helix and its sister companies, Beta Helix and Delta Helix, acquire, manage and operate Oil and Natural Gas wells and properties across the state. Our expertise is in the acquisition of distressed properties and improving production and efficiency on these properties through well service, repair and recompletion techniques. We operate conventional and horizontal wells in over twenty counties across the state of West Virginia. Alpha Helix Energy's goal is to continue growing through acquisitions in order to provide a sustainable energy source to West Virginia and its surrounding areas for generations to come.

Mineral/Royalty Owners

Alpha Helix Energy, Beta Helix Energy, and Delta Helix Energy understand the importance of working with our partners including the surface and mineral owners. Maintaining good relationships with these partners is integral to operating a successful oil and natural gas business in the state of West Virginia. Please feel free to contact us with any questions you may have or provide simple contact information changes at support@alphahelixenergy.com.

If you are a royalty/mineral owner and have a transfer of ownership that you would like to report, please gather the information as depicted below and send it to us via email at support@alphahelixenergy.com or by mail to our offices as listed in the contact section.

Circumstance Required Documentation
Deceased owner with a will (testate)
  • Copy of the probated and recorded will
  • Copy of the Death Certificate
  • List of the heirs' contact information and W-9's
Deceased owner without a will (intestate)
  • Estate Administration Documentation
  • Copy of the Death Certificate
  • List of the heirs' contact information and W-9's
Deed/Conveyance of property
  • Copy of the recorded deed from the county wherein the property is located
  • W-9 for the new owner
